The land in San Jacinto County

San Jacinto sits west of Polk County across the lake. The terrain is similar — sandy uplands, lakefront slopes, creek-bottom clay near tributaries. The west side of Lake Livingston has its own subdivision character: lots of recreational acreage, weekend properties, and lake homes.
