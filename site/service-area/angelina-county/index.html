Lufkin, Diboll, Huntington, Zavalla, Hudson, and the surrounding rural acreage.

The land in Angelina County

Angelina is pine-dominant, with some of the most productive pine ground in Texas. Multiple major mills are in or near the county, which keeps stumpage prices competitive. Soils run sandy across the uplands, with clay near the Angelina River corridor.

Specialty here

If you're selling timber in Angelina County, competitive bidding is even more important than usual — multiple mills nearby means real market depth, but only if your sale is bid out. A direct sale here is leaving money on the table almost by definition.
