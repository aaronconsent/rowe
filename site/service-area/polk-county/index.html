The land in Polk County

Polk County is mixed — sandy Piney Woods uplands across most of the county transitioning to clay creek bottoms toward the Trinity River and to sandy lakefront soils around Lake Livingston. Pine plantations dominate the upland timber base; bottomland hardwood corridors line the creeks. We work all of it.
